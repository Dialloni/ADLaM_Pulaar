Minimum clear space = 25% of mark height

For an 80 px mark that's 20 px of breathing room on every side. The x unit is simply mark height ÷ 4. Use this same rule in every context — side-by-side layouts, lockups, and especially inside dense UIs like the app sidebar.

Do & Don't
Do
Gradient on dark — the default. Use on #0a0a0a or #0e0e0e.
Do
Mono ink on light — when printed or on a light UI, use the single-color mono variant.
Do
Ink on brand fill — on a pink or orange block, reverse to solid #0a0a0a.
Don't
Never on busy imagery without a chip or plate behind. The gradient dissolves.
Don't
Never rotate or skew. The Ga Hook sits upright. Always.
Don't
Never recolor. The gradient and the orange terminal dot are fixed.
